About iSSM

What are we?

The iSSM was established in 2017, it has a group of professional engineers having many years of practical experience in the field of power generation, transmission, distribution and sustainable energy solutions. Muhammad Taheruzzaman and couple of Engineers were involved and played an important role in the development of electricity infrastructure development green empowerment in Bangladesh. The consultancy firm is based on collection of many years of the founders knowledge with a blend with the highly qualified individuals who have joined the firm since its establishment. We also offers various Training on renewable energy integration, Power System, and Energy Audit.

Our Work Experience

Sustainability Consultant 2017-2018

: Led energy audits for commercial and industrial clients, identifying areas for efficiency improvements and cost savings. Conducted comprehensive life cycle assessments and developed environmental product declarations to quantify environmental impacts. Collaborated with cross-functional teams to implement energy-saving measures and decarbonization projects, achieving significant reductions in carbon footprint and energy consumption. Provided expertise in sustainability reporting, helping clients align with ESG criteria and develop SDG action plans for meaningful social and environmental impact.

Climate Change Analyst 2019-2020

Researched and analyzed climate change trends and impacts on various sectors. Developed climate change adaptation and mitigation strategies for government agencies and private organizations. Utilized data-driven approaches to monitor and evaluate climate change initiatives, informing policy recommendations and resilience-building measures. Facilitated workshops and training sessions on climate change awareness and action planning.

Renewable Energy Engineer 2018-2019

Designed and implemented renewable energy systems, including solar PV, wind, and biomass projects. Conducted feasibility studies and site assessments to optimize renewable energy deployment. Managed installation and commissioning processes, ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations. Provided ongoing support for renewable energy projects, including performance monitoring and maintenance optimization.

Sustainability Reporting Specialist 2019-2022

Developed and implemented sustainability reporting frameworks for multinational corporations, focusing on ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) criteria. Led data collection and analysis efforts to track key sustainability metrics and indicators. Collaborated with stakeholders to create compelling sustainability reports that communicate organizational achievements and progress towards sustainability goals. Provided guidance on integrating sustainability practices into business operations and decision-making processes.

Energy Management Consultant 2010-2022

Advised clients on energy management strategies to optimize consumption and reduce environmental impact.
